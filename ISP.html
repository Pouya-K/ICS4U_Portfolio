One of the largest issues in the world is homelessness and shelter insecurity. In African developing countries such as Ethiopia, there are over 150,000 street children, a million in Egypt, and over 250,000 in Kenya (Ogutu, 2020). More than 80 million African children lack access to healthy shelter, and 16 million of these children are living on the streets (Ochola, n.d.). Habitat Hero is designed to not only educate smaller children on shelter security, but also how to survive if they ever find themselves in that unfortunate situation. Teaching kids the basic knowledge required to build makeshift houses will have a huge impact on them and their families due to the need for homes.
